About Me

My name is David Coelho, I’m 31 years old and hold a degree in Multimedia. Still, it was through managing residential care homes for the elderly that I built my professional path. For nearly ten years, I led teams, made complex decisions and stayed close to people in vulnerable moments of life. That’s where I learned that management isn’t just about structure — it’s about care, listening, and acting with humanity.
I’m someone who values meaningful work. I enjoy thinking, learning, and walking. I’m currently investing in my studies in Economics, with the aim of deepening my understanding of the world and contributing more consciously and meaningfully to society. It’s allways possible to improve the structures we live and work in with balance, fairness and humanity.
Meanwhile, I continue to look for challenges that push me forward, alongside people and projects that truly matter.
